Inpatient & Ambulatory Language Access Plan

This language access plan enables us to provide equitable, consistent, and easily accessible support to patients and families who speak a language other than English (LOE) by giving:

  • Clear and simple expectations

  • Proper training

  • Translation guideline

  • Readily available assistance when needed
