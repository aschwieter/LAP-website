Language Access Quality Improvement

Any language access quality improvement projects should involve staff from Language Access Services. Contact interpreterservices@cchmc.org for support.

In creating this plan, we compiled a list of possible improvement projects.

There is also a Spanish-Speaking Family Forum to discuss issues and co-create solutions. Contact Karla Eysoldt to get on the agenda.
