Language Access Training

Many areas include language access in their orientation and onboarding.

All employees are given a blue badge card with basic information about accessing interpreters (catalog item 124763).

Additional training is available from Language Access Services. Contact interpreterservices@cchmc.org for support.

On-demand training includes:

  • TEACH BACK TRAINING NEEDED

  • LANGUAGE ACCESS BASICS NEEDED

  • Ambulatory:

    • Access Services "Bridging the Experience Gap Utilizing Language Access Services"
